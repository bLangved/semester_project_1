Explore together

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we’re committed to making science accessible to all.

Free entrance!

The entrance is free for all. We believe all people regardless of background should be able to learn about the wonders of science, and get the oppertunity to educate themself as they see fit.

Guided tours

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

We want everyone to have fun at the museum, so of course we have wheelchair accessibility ramps, audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Exhibition Spaces

Come explore our exhibitions with range from Cosmology, robotics, and so much more. There should be something fun and interesting for everyone!
